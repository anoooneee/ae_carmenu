Modern, intuitive, and sleek Vehicle Control Menu for your FiveM server. Take full control of your vehicle with a clean, user-friendly interface.

---

## ✨ Features
- 🚪 **Door Control** – Easily open/close individual doors (Front/Rear).
- 🪟 **Window Control** – Quick toggles for vehicle windows.
- 💺 **Seat Management** – Seamlessly switch seats within the vehicle.
- ⚡ **Vehicle Status** – Monitor and manage the state of your car.
- 🎨 **Modern UI** – A stylish and responsive design that fits any server theme.
- ⚙️ **Highly Configurable** – Fully adjustable to suit your server's needs.
